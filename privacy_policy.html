Privacy Policy

Your Rights and Choices:

  • You have the following rights and choices regarding your personal information:
    • You can delete or modify your user data at any time.
    • You can opt-in or opt-out of data sharing or marketing communications.

Updates to this Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or through the Application. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@stash.com.
